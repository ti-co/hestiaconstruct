With a technology-agnostic environment, we are looking for ambitious and self-driven Front-End Developers to join our team.

 

Spark is new. We’re forward-thinking and doing a great job challenging the status quo across the software and product development and delivery business. By approaching our customers’ challenges differently, some are saying we’re reimagining the space! We love to hear that, and with a commitment to growing our team with diverse talent, diverse thinking, and promoting equality across our business, we are looking for people to join our team that feel the same or, at least, see an opportunity to do things differently.

 

We describe ourselves through Radical Honesty; Empathy over Ego; Energy Management vs Time Management; and a mindset that we're 'In it Together'. With a go get it done attitude - Nothing is impossible at Spark.

 

Committed to growing our team with diverse talent, diverse thinking, and promoting equality across our business, we are looking for people who is ready to grow!

 

Specifically, we see your time is spent:

  • Getting involved and participating in the overall application lifecycle of a project, with a focus on coding and debugging.
  • Ensuring the customer is at the front and centre of each thought process.
  • Working hand-in-hand with our delivery manager, architect and designers, defining and discussing technical and design requirements.
  • Writing clean and sustainable code that you are proud of in the development of large scale, high performing systems.
  • Defining and writing fully automated tests.
  • Staying up to date on best practices and technologies. (we provide our team with opportunities to up-skill with new technologies and move along their development into cloud-based technologies).
  • Ongoing collaboration with Spark's team of designers, developers, and strategists.

 

What will pique our interest is someone that might have:

  • 3+ years of experience as a Front-End Engineer.
  • Commercial experience of DevOps and CI/CD.
  • Understanding of data privacy.
  • 3+ years of experience with programming languages and frameworks such as Node, HTML/CSS, Angular, React.
  • Excellent communication skills, fluent written and spoken English.

  • We're a fully remote-first work environment, though you're always welcome and encouraged to join us at our co-working spaces in our Dublin City Centre HQ or Development Centres in Malaga or Lisbon.
